Let’s Get Dad Moving

It’s Father’s Day and we’re encouraging all the dads to move more! After all, heart disease and diabetes are two of the top six causes of male deaths, and both are preventable with regular exercise. The best thing any father can do for his son or daughter is to be healthy and happy. The second best? To pass on the desire to keep active through regular exercise.

You know what they say – a family that FitStars together, stays FIT together! So come on, put dad to the test and do a FitStar workout together. We’ve made all of our Freestyle Sessions FREE this weekend so there’s no excuse.

It’s easy! Just do your next Program workout together or try one of our 19 fun Freestyle Sessions. Short on time? Give the 7-Minute Workout a go, and tack on 10-Minute Abs for an extra core-burn. Looking for something to bring out the competitive spirit? Give Training Camp a go – it’s an intense 25 minute workout that helps build endurance. Need something a little mellower? Our new Greatist Wherever Workout is a great lower impact workout that can literally be done…anywhere!

And if dad likes FitStar, give him a FREE month through our Referral Program. It’s easy, just tell him to follow these simple instructions. Be sure to post pictures to our Facebook page of you and your dad (or kids!) exercising with #FitStarStories.

From the FitStar team to your family, we wish you a Happy Father’s Day!
