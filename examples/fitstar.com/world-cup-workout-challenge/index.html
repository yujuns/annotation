FitStar 10-Day World Cup Workout Challenge

We want to help you get in soccer star shape so we put together the FitStar World Cup Workout Challenge! This fun, 10-Day program uses several of our Freestyle Sessions and is designed to kick up your fitness and make you feel great. You’ll already be watching teams like England vs. Italy and Ghana vs. United States duke it out, so why not workout between matches. You’ll thank us later!

All you need is our FitStar app and a little motivation. The Challenge runs June 13-22 and every Freestyle Session will be FREE for all users during this time.

Here’s everything you need to know about our 10-Day World Cup Workout Challenge:

Dates: The FitStar Challenge runs June 13 – June 22.

Download: Download FitStar Personal Trainer for FREE from the App Store. All Freestyle Sessions will be unlocked to use during our World Cup Workout Challenge.

Details: Follow our workout plan above and do the Freestyle Session(s) listed each day. You can also join the FitStar 10-Day World Cup Workout Challenge on Facebook for daily updates and motivation.

Two lucky Challenge participants will be given an autographed copy of Tony Gonzalez’s book “The All-Pro Diet.” Entry details to come!

Share the 10-Day World Cup Workout Challenge and invite friends. The more the merrier!

We want to hear from you so make sure to tell us how the Challenge is going! Use #FitStarChallenge in your Facebook posts and tweets. You can also leave a comment below.

Keep Moving!

-Team FitStar
