]]> WE are a passionate team creating a next-generation mobile fitness platform and are hell-bent on crafting products that inspire millions of people to get fit and live healthier lives.  If the idea of disrupting the $100+ billion fitness industry, shipping world class products employing breakthrough technology in a scrappy, low-ego, high performance environment appeals to you, read on…

YOU are skilled at building consumer-facing mobile products that delight customers on a daily basis.    You are passionate about creating user experiences that are beautiful, intuitive, and unique. You think about the big picture, the brand, and most importantly, the user from concept to execution.

You love working with a team on a mission, and are expert in collaborating with whip-smart engineers, product and business folks to find elegant but practical solutions to design challenges. You love creating rough prototypes to vet ideas, and have maniacal attention to detail in the finished product.

You’re a clear and articulate communicator. You’re deliberate about your design philosophies, but rely on feedback from users and the team to help iterate. You thrive in a dynamic, fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Role

The Product Designer will be responsible for creating simple, elegant user interaction design on one or more FitStar products, through all phases of a product lifecycle. This role will collaborate heavily with other designers, product managers and engineers.

Responsibilities

  • Lead interaction design for our mobile and web experiences
  • Define the user journey and goals and build experiences that deliver on them
  • Design conceptual schematics focusing on end-to-end user experience
  • Create clickable mockups and lightweight prototypes to communicate and validate design decisions
  • Create visual design treatments and assets in collaboration with visual designer
  • Collaborate with product management to develop and understand key user stories
  • Participate in the design, observation, and analysis of user research

Ideal Candidates Will Have

  • 5+ years experience in design roles in fast-paced environments
  • Shipped at least 1 iOS application
  • Portfolio demonstrating strong interaction and visual design principles
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Experience defining design process

Bonus points for

  • Previous experience in a fast-paced startup environment
  • User research experience

Portfolio submission required for this position.
