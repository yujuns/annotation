We set out to create an elegant and uncomplicated fitness app that allows you to be the best version of you. Here are some things you might be wondering.

How much space do I need?

If you can lay on the floor with arms and legs outstretched, you’ll have enough space for even our most challenging Sessions.

Do I need equipment?

Nope. An iOS7 device and gear you can sweat in is all you need to do a FitStar Session. FitStar is all about making sure fitness fits into your schedule no matter where you are.

So, how much?

FitStar Basic is always free. No questions asked. If exclusive workouts, unlimited access, nutritional guidance, and HD video gets you moving, our Premium plans, starting as low as $4.99, might be for you. See our pricing plans.

How long are the Sessions?

Our shortest Session is 7 minutes. Looking for something longer and more challenging? We’ve got those too. Whatever works for you in the time you have available, FitStar will challenge you.
