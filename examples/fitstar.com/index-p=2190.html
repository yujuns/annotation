Get Running With Runtensity

We’re committed to keeping your fitness routine fresh, and part of that means partnering with other companies that complement the FitStar experience.

Lots of FitStars love running, and this time of year is a great time to cross-train to get those muscles ready for action when Spring arrives. To that end, we’ve teamed up with Runtastic to ensure that your winter workouts are fun and engaging.

Runtastic is a great run-tracking app used by millions of people around the world. We put our heads together with Runtastic fitness coach Lunden Souza to develop our latest Freestyle Session – Runtensity. This workout focuses on endurance, explosiveness, and core to give you a full-body workout that will prepare you to reach your running and training goals.

To check out Runtensity on FitStar, just fire it up on your iPhone or iPad and head over to the Freestyle area.  Don’t have FitStar? Download it for free. Don’t have Runtastic? Get it here.

With FitStar and Runtastic, you are ready to take on 2014!

– Team FitStar
