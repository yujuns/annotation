]]> Hi All –

Hard to believe, but it has been a year since we officially launched the first version of FitStar.

It started with a simple idea: by making fitness more convenient, inspirational and personal, we could do a better job of getting people moving — whatever their current state of fitness. In short, we wanted to put a personal trainer on your iPhone.

When we starting looking into consumer fitness options, we saw that DVD workouts really haven’t changed since the original Jane Fonda workouts from 1982. While some do a good job of showing people what to do, they’re static experiences that don’t evolve and change with you.  We saw the same phenomenon with other video-based fitness apps and utilities that prevail in the marketplace. Out of this realization, and a lot of hard work, FitStar was born.

Our mission is simple: to inspire people to live healthier lives. And our goal is to revolutionize the way people exercise and deliver a personalized, digital fitness experience that could be used anytime, anywhere.

We couldn’t have picked a better debut partner and fitness guru than Tony Gonzalez. There’s nobody we could think of that’s lived a more authentic life of health – it shows with his longevity and success on and off the field. We’re grateful he’s a part of our FitStar story, and we’re just getting started.

The best part of this amazing journey? It’s working. People are using FitStar and getting real results – while improving their health and well-being. We’ve heard great stories from people around the world of pounds lost and confidence gained. That’s what keeps US motivated.

Watch our “Year in Review” video for a walk down memory lane.

Music credit: Coldplay

I’m humbled to say that, to date, well over 1 million people have downloaded the first app developed for our platform: FitStar Personal Trainer, and more than 150 million calories have been collectively burned by our FitStars. As you can see below, we have some really cool, fun stats earned by our users around the world. So today, we celebrate a lot of hard work by our internal team – but also our dedicated FitStars everywhere. We couldn’t have done it without you.

Thank you for joining us on this journey and we hope you continue to get as much out of FitStar as we put in. There is much more to come.

Mike

CEO/Co-founder

]]> We heard about Sam’s “7 Day Challenge with FitStar” when he tweeted about it earlier this month. Being a shy and introverted person means he’s not as comfortable in the gym setting. We love that Sam gave FitStar a shot and asked him to share his experience. Spoiler alert: It’s been over 7 days and he’s still using FitStar!

Here are five facts you need to know about FitStar: Sam Matrouh.

1) What’s your name and where do you live?

My name is Sam and I’m a Sydney-based web designer and developer.

2) How long have you been using FitStar and what Program do you use?

I received one free week of FitStar Premium so I went on a 7-day challenge to try it out using the Get Strong Program.

3) What is your FitStar story?

Being in a long-term relationship with a foodie and chef isn’t exactly the most diet-friendly thing in this world. Imagine having restaurant quality food, three meals a day, six days a week and then going restaurant hopping on the 7th day. As we settled in and got comfortable in our relationship, so did the extra couple kilos around my waist.

Being a big muscular guy has never been on my priority list, but I take fitness seriously and the beginnings of a beer belly in the mirror seemed like a pretty good sign that I should do something about it.

So I started going to the gym, but two months down the track and a few hundred dollars later, I realized that the gym experience was not for me. This was mainly due to the lack of proper training, overcomplicated equipment and my introverted personality.

I won’t bore you with the full story here, but if you have a few spare minutes you can read it on medium.com. The strangest part of my 7-day journey was the day I stepped on the scale. I had lost .5kg (that’s about 1 pound), but I felt like I’ve done nothing to deserve this. That was an amazing feeling.

4) Why is FitStar a good fit for you?

One of the best parts of using FitStar was that I didn’t have to have a routine. It took zero planning on my end. I just had to put on my shoes and press play. Everything else was taken care of.

5) What have you gained by using FitStar?

It didn’t take me long to realize that FitStar was just the thing for me. I had an intelligent personal trainer that challenged me as I progressed. I didn’t need any equipment to start, and best of all, I got to do everything from the comfort of my own home. No uncomfortable overcrowded gyms with intimidatingly fit and social people.

Generally if I use something for a week or so and I’m still excited about it, it’s a solid indication that I’m not going to change my mind anytime soon. It’s been just over two weeks since I started using FitStar and well, I guess I’m not stopping anytime soon.

Way to go Sam – we hope you keep us posted on your progress!
