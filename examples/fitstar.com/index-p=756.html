New Friends

It has been just two weeks since the worldwide release of our first product, FitStar: Tony Gonzalez.  The early response has been really positive, and we’ve loved hearing from people about how they’re using the product.  Earlier this week, we pulled some stats and were blown away by the fact that we’re already in 145 countries, and that FitStars have completed over 500,000 pushups and burned over 4,000,000 calories.  Amazing.

Today, we’re excited to welcome our friends at Trinity Ventures to the FitStar family.  As we’ve just announced, the team at Trinity is the lead investor in our latest round of funding, in which several of our existing investors participated.  Trinity’s Ajay Chopra will be joining our Board of Directors and will help support us in our mission.

From our first introduction to Trinity several months ago, it was clear we had a shared vision about the future of consumer health and fitness.  One in which the blending of compelling content, mobile-first sensibilities, and a platform approach will allow for a wide array of experiences to help millions of people improve their health.

More than this, we felt an instant chemistry between our  cultures, and a singular focus on making awesome products for our FitStar athletes.

We feel incredibly fortunate to have this additional support to grow our team (we’re hiring!), accelerate the pace of our product releases, and build new partnerships with brands, device makers, and fitness luminaries.

Okay, back to building.

Mike

ceo/co-founder
