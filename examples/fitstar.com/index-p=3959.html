FitStar Stories: Laura Jenkins

Laura has been using FitStar for five months and has lost 23 pounds and four dress sizes! But most importantly, she has gained confidence.

Here are five facts you need to know about FitStar: Laura Jenkins.

1) What’s your name and where do you live?

My name is Laura Jenkins and I’m from Camano Island WA.

2) How long have you been using FitStar and what Program do you use?

I’ve been using FitStar for 5 months and I started with Get Lean. Now, I’m using Get Strong.

3) What is your FitStar story? 

I’ve lost 23 lbs and went from a size 14 to size 6! I am a small business owner, so usually I go 2-3 weeks in between visits with clients. They go nuts every time I see them. I’ve even heard people say they didn’t recognize me. So, thank YOU FitStar for this amazing program.

4) Why is FitStar is a good fit for you?

FitStar is so convenient with my schedule and 4 year old son. I don’t have to find childcare, and I can workout from home or on vacation. There is very little cost. And the workouts are simple enough to follow.

5) What have you gained by using FitStar?

The biggest thing I’ve gained by using FitStar is confidence and self worth. I do feel much healthier and stronger. I can fit into clothing I never thought I would be able to. I’m empowered to become the person I’ve always wanted to be not only on the inside, but on the outside!!

Way to go Laura!
