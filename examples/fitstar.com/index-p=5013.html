FitStar Stories: Hasaneen Yousif

It’s been both fun and motivating for our team to hear stories from our users around the world. And this weeks FitStar is all the way from Iraq. In the few months Hasaneen has been using FitStar, he’s lost 5% body fat! He also credits FitStar with helping him gain more strength and endurance.

Here are five facts you need to know about FitStar: Hasaneen Yousif.

1. What’s your name and where do you live?

I am Hasaneen Yousif from Iraq.

2. How long have you been using FitStar and what Program do you use?

I have been using FitStar for about 2-3 months and I’m doing the Get Strong program.

3. What’s your FitStar story?

I went from 20% total body fat to 15% body fat.

4. Why is FitStar is a good fit for you?

Since I started working out with FitStar, I’ve seen more results than my year and a half going to the gym and spending large amounts of money!

5. What have you gained by using FitStar?

I have gained more strength. Thanks to FitStar I can now run a football field five times and still have energy to train. Also, I just wanted to say that this app really changed my life and I’d like to thank all the people that worked on creating such wonderful and fun way to train.

Way to go Hasaneen and thank you – we are so glad you get as much out of FitStar Personal Trainer as we put in!

We’d love to hear from you. Share your story or before/after picture with us on Facebook or Twitter with#FitStarStories. You can also email us at: stories@fitstar.com.
