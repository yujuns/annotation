Summer is Coming – Get That Body Moving with Beach Booty

We’ve helped you strengthen your core with 10-Minute Abs, and now it’s time to get that butt into swimsuit shape with Beach Booty. This 20(ish)-minute Freestyle Session will leave your backside sore and your legs, arms, back, and core ready for all your summertime fun.

With Moves like Jump Lunges, One-Leg Floor Bridge, and Star Skaters, your lower body will look and feel great as you surf the waves, run the shore, or just soak up the sunshine in your favorite bikini or boardshorts…while you’re checking out the other bikinis and boardshorts. And just in case you’ve got more than a couple summertime BBQs on tap, we’ve got you covered with sets of Side Planks, Mountain Climbers, and Bicycle kicks to keep that core in check. With 16 sizzling Moves, you’ve got a total body workout in less time than it takes to watch the Baywatch intro 12 times.
