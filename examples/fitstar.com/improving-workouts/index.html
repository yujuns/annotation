A Better FitStar Through Big Data

After six months of helping FitStars burn more than 50,000,000 collective calories, we’ve learned a lot about what works and what doesn’t. Hot on the heels of our recent 2.0 launch, we’ve looked at all the data, and we’re ready to launch an update to our fitness progression algorithms.

Starting today, you’ll begin to see refinements to individual Moves, reps, and sets as you progress through your selected Program. Moves that previously only existed in Freestyle Sessions will be added to your weekly routines (with more to come), and you may find your Sessions loaded with a few more push-ups or a smaller set of lunges than before. Many of you will be happy to know that we’ve expanded our cardio offerings as well!

All of these refinements come together to better keep you in an ideal fitness zone – dialing up the challenge when your sets are “Too Easy” and toning it down when you can’t lift those high knees high enough. These refinements will help keep you on track toward your fitness goals.

The great thing about FitStar is how dynamically we can make adjustments based on the massive amount of aggregated, anonymous data from our community we’ve collected. Every rep completed, every set marked “Brutal,” and every Session rating helped us to tune the progression algorithm and further customize and refine your workout experience. So keep rating those Moves and Sessions – not just for the betterment of your own fitness, but for the good of FitStars everywhere!

We’re excited to be a part of your health and fitness routines, and we can’t wait to hear what you think about our latest improvements.

Have fun, and make it happen!

- Dave

vp of engineering/co-founder
