Alpine Blitz is Here

We know that keeping things fresh is the key to staying with any fitness routine, so we’re busy working on new Moves and Sessions to get 2014 off to a fit start. Our newest Freestyle Session, Alpine Blitz, is now available for all FitStar Premium subscribers.

Whether you’re hitting the mountain or hitting the mall, this 17 minute Session has you covered with 7 all-new bodyweight moves and a focus on legs, core, cardio, and overall balance and stability – perfect for tackling moguls or fitting into your swimsuit when you trade the snowboard for the surfboard.

Here’s a list of all the new Moves in Alpine Blitz:

  • Bicycle Kicks
  • Jump Lunges
  • Spiderman Pushups
  • Squat Walk
  • Star Crawl
  • Star Skaters
  • Superman Planks

While our handcrafted Freestyle Sessions don’t dynamically change like those in your chosen Program (e.g., Daily Dose, Get Strong, etc.), they’re still a great way to get in a quick workout or target a specific muscle group while progressing towards your fitness goals. And very soon, we’ll be adding these Moves into all of the Programs to add variety and keep you on track.

Alpine Blitz is one of our Premium Member offerings.  If you’re using FitStar Basic, now’s a great time to upgrade to Premium while our Annual Pass is 40% off. FitStar Premium grants you unlimited access to our entire range of Freestyle Sessions and Programs like Get Lean and Get Strong.

To take advantage of this limited-time offer, simply launch FitStar on your mobile device, click on the menu icon, select Programs, and select Get it All with Premium.

Give Alpine Blitz a try and let us know your thoughts. We hope to see you all at the top of the mountain!

Keep moving,
