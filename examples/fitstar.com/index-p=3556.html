Spring into Summer Shape with FitStar – and Win!

As the weather warms up, FitStar wants to help you Spring into Summer shape!

That’s why we’re giving away the No Excuses Ultimate Prize Pack and sharing FitStar Stories to help motivate you.

SPRING INTO SUMMER SHAPE GIVEAWAY

The No Excuses Ultimate Prize Pack is loaded with all the tools you need to look and feel your best. It’s filled with an iPad Air pre-loaded with one year access to FitStar Premium, FitStar T-shirt, $100 Whole Foods gift card, Nike shoes and a consultation with a FitStar trainer!

How to enter to win our No Excuses Ultimate Prize Pack:

  • -Simply click here to enter!
  • -Increase your odds of winning by sharing and tweeting out your entry and tagging @FitStar.

Our Spring into Summer Shape Giveaway runs through June 15th and one lucky FitStar will win.

SHARE YOUR FITSTAR STORY

We’re inspired every day by the work you put in and love hearing your successes! So we’re officially launching #FitStarStories as a way to share your stories with the entire community.

Each week, we’ll feature a new FitStar Story on our blog and social media communities, plus show our appreciation by giving the featured FitStar a year of FitStar Premium and FitStar T-shirt.

Have you been using FitStar for awhile and want to show off your results? Send a picture now. New to FitStar? Take a photo today and send in your image when you start seeing results. Earn bragging rights – and some cool FitStar swag.

There are three easy ways to join the conversation and share your success:

  1. -Tweet us a photo of your before and after with #FitStarStories.
  2. -Upload it to our Facebook wall with #FitStarStories.
  3. -Email us at stories@fitstar.com.

SPRING INTO SUMMER WITH NEW FITSTAR WORKOUTS

We recently launched two new FreeStyle workouts that will help kick start your Spring workouts: Rock Steady – our first low impact Session, and Beach Booty - a 20-minute total body workout that blasts your butt, legs, arms, back, abs and obliques. Try pairing either of these with 10-Minute Abs for an ideal 30 minute sweat session.

Don’t forget to like us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter.
